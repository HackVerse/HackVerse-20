What is Hackverse ?

HackVerse, an idea visualised by IE NITK, seeks to encourage enthusiastic minds to brainstorm on solutions for the most challenging issues in technical aspects. We provide a platform for innovations, where developers can test and showcase their potential to the best of their abilities

About NITK

National Institute of Technology Karnataka or NITK is one of the premier engineering institutions of the country. Situated near Mangalore along the coast of the Arabian Sea, NITK is home to some of the brightest young minds of the country engaged in a wide array of disciplines. With a vibrant and robust student body, NITK has been the launchpad for people with great ideas that have grown to be very successful ventures including Practo, Purpule, SaveMonk among others. This year, we celebrate our Diamond Jubilee. To commemorate 60 years of NITK, IE NITK aims to provide students throughout the country an opportunity to do the same with Hackverse‚Äô19.

About IE

IE NITK is one of the 6 exclusive clubs in NITK, aiming to increase technical exposure to all its members. Our previous Hackathon, Digital Hackathon which was conducted last year, sparked our ingenious students to transpire cutting edge solutions to various problem statements. Other events like Spark, a small scale version of TED talks and KOTH, a challenge filled series, sought to bring out the interest within our freshman.

FAQs


  • 1. Is this hackathon an online event or offline?

    HackVerse is an offline event and as a participant you need to be present at NITK campus.

  • 2. Is there any registration fee?

    No. Registrations are completely free of cost.

  • 3. Do you provide accommodation and fooding?

  • 4. Are there any rules ?

    There are as such no particular rules to be followed. But all the participants do need to follow a code of conduct.

  • 5. How big can a team be?

    You can form teams of up to 3 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.

  • 6. What should I do if I do not have any team yet?

    Just fill out the application form and we will match you with the best team possible during Pre-hackathon meeting(Can we do this)

  • 7. What are the Perks of participating in this Hackathon?

    Hackathon Provide hands on experience with the experts from different field working on the Projects like Blockchain, AI, Machine Learning, etc. This will help to give a head start if any one want to make project on the field. Along with that the participant will be provided with cool swags, stickers and refreshments to keep them up.Also our sponsors will be providing problem statements and if team works on the problem statements, they may get company prizes and recruitment benefits.

  • 8. What should I bring?

    Bring your student ID, laptop, chargers. A pillow and sleeping bag may be useful as well. Please leave anything illegal at home.

  • 9. I don't have a CS background. Can I still participate?

    Yes. Creativity is independent of your technical skills. You can learn the tools and technologies anytime.

  • 10. What is the criterion of selection?

    You will be judged on the quality, innovation, feasibility and impact of your idea/plan of action, project implementation.

  • 11. Can I start working on my hack before the event?

    No. In the interest of fairness, students should not be working on their projects before the hackathon begins and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. However, you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand!

  • 12. I have a question related to hackathon which is not listed above. What should I do?

    You can always contact us at hackverse@nitk.edu.in
